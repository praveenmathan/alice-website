How it works

Download the Alice Care app from the Apple App Store or Google Play.

Signup for an account and complete a profile for who is to receive home care services.

Select the tasks you need performed and pick your schedule.

Alice Care will match you with an available provider and alert you when they are on their way.

You pay for tasks completed and will be automatically charged to the card of file with Alice Care.

FAQ

How can I trust the Alice Care Provider that you send?

We conduct thorough background checks on all of our providers including Live Scan fingerprinting and all of our providers are at a minimum registered as Home Care Aides with the California Department of Public Health.

How much does it cost?

With Alice Care, you only select the tasks you need done, and pay per task, not by the hour, and there are no hourly commitments. You will receive an estimate of the services total cost before you finalize your request.

Who do I contact if I need help?

We have a 24/7 customer care phone line available to answer any questions.
